Skynet Corporation is a relatively new company founded in 2008, in Calcutta, India. We at Skynet are untiringly working towards developing cost effective and simple web applications for the present Internet –era, so that the benefits of a connected world goes out to everybody from the technologically challenged to the hardcore geek.

A brief summary of the products that we have developed so far:

  • Simple games that can be run with simple hardware including even a Pentium 1 computer running DOS.
  • An online banking solution called OBS that delivers banking products and financial services over the internet.
  • Our premium offering is the Online Shopping Mall or OSM which can be very easily tailored to the needs of any retailer wishing to have a web presence.

We are different from our competitors in the very sense of simplicity of our products. We always strive to manufacture applications and provide services that can be enjoyed by virtually anyone. We offer this simplicity at a very low cost. We also strive to use as much free and open source software as we can so that we can reuse the components to bring a new product faster to the market as well as provide the product at an affordable price. And because of the simplicity of design our products are highly scalable and extensible. So our products can evolve along with the business of our clients.

Our product development team consists of Surjyendu Ray, Sandip Shaw, Suvendu Bhattacharyya and Souvik Sett. This team is highly innovative and brings new ideas to the market in a competitive package. Our dedication more or less compensates for our lack of experience and we are gathering invaluable experience as we speak. We always are all ears for our customers’ aspirations and ideas and they form the framework for every venture that we step into.

We are proficient in application development for the Web as well as business process reengineering for companies who had not have a web presence till date. Our world can be visualized at www.skynet.in and also at osmlite.googlecode.com. We can be contacted via email at surjray@gmail.com
